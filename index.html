What do we have to offer? Well, everything that youth needs to be proactive!

Personalized opportunities

We save users´ time and ensure they find what is interesting for them by offering the most relevant opportunities recommendations.

A big network of active youth

Users can share ideas and knowledge with peers from all over the country, which can give rise to new social initiatives and strong friendships.

Fast sharing of updates on projects

Users will not have to directly communicate with the moderator to publish their projects on the platform and post updates on them.

Recieve personalized recommendations

User indicates their interests, age, and other preferences while registering on the platform. The algorithm will provide opportunities that correspond to their preferences. Once the person finds an opportunity appealing to him, he can add this to the list of opportunities. The deadline of the application (if any) will be automatically added to the platform’s calendar. The participant gets a notification as the deadline for completing the application approaches. Opportunities may be adjusted to the specific city and day or period of time.

Connect with like-minded peers

If users want to orginize their own social projects, they can post a project summary to find volunteers from all over the world to make these projects! Moreover, users indicate projects\camps they participated in, and it can be shown on his profile. In this way, we will create communities of alumni, where they can find their old friends or share the experience of participating in indifferent activities. So when someone wants to learn more about particular project, the person can easily talk with the project's alumni.

Develop own social projects

Organizations and users can post their opportunities on the platform to attract more participants. Many projects are done not only by non-profits but also by the youth themselves. So we want to help youth develop their projects further by giving them an online sharing space for free advertising.
